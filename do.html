Try something you have never done before in your life! Our unique activities are a must-do when you are at the resort.

Horse Ridingt

Their beauty is striking. Noble, gentle and peaceful, don’t miss the opportunity to get up close and personal with our naked horses. Each one has his/her own personality… just ask our staff for their stories!

Leaf Spa

Leaf Spa, hidden in a forest glade, is an opportunity to immerse into the very heart of nature. A full range of treatments are available, from foot massage to Thai massage, to full body aromatherapy massage with therapeutic oils. While the massage therapists work their magic, the serenity of the bamboo forest casts its spell.

Woodworking

Have the itch to create something? Cut, design, and polish your masterpiece. With our woodworking workshop, turn the beautiful wood into something you’ll treasure.

Yoga

There is no better way to start your day than with an outdoor yoga class on the tranquil terrace of the Mandela House at valley Stables.

Swimming

A resort experience isn’t complete without getting your feet wet, literally! Splash around in one (or all!) of the three swimming pools on the resort.

Hiking & Biking

Get close to nature! Suitable for all ages and fitness levels, these trails are designed to introduce you to the unique facilities and surrounding vistas.

Pottery

Get your hands dirty as you learn the craft and mould your own creation.

Golf

Nothing says “holiday” like a round of golf in a gorgeous location. At valley Stables we have a beautiful golf course in our backyard.

Fishing

Enjoy the beauty of fishing year-round at our private pond. Surrounded by the peaceful bamboo forest, all you have to do is sit back, relax and hope for the big catch!

Little Shoots Kids Club

Step into a kid’s paradise! From the outside swing, endless toys, and creative activities, kids at naked Stables can busy themselves for hours at the kids club.
