With 3 different outlets at the resort, as well as in-villa options, try them all during your stay.We dare you!

Farmhouse

The Farmhouse offers casual all-day dining – simple, fresh and wholesome. It is a focal point at naked Stables, situated above The Big Lawn and next to The Stables; it is here that guests gravitate toward when not tucked away in their forest homes.

Kikaboni

With only 9 tables, kikaboni offers the fresh concept of ‘private dining in nature’. Featuring European-style cuisine with local flavours, the seasonal menu features high quality imported and locally sourced ingredients, with some produce coming from our very own naked Farm.

Valley Cafeteria

Valley Cafeteria is casual, pool-side dining. The menu features a blend of Asian and Western favourites from all over the world. Don’t restrict yourself to a table in the restaurant; a meal can be set up just about anywhere in the resort. For refreshments, there’s a bar serving beer, wine and seasonal cocktails.
