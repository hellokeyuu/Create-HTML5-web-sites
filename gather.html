Corporate Activities

Valley Stables offers the absence of distractions, venues for team building, seclusion for break out sessions and flexible catering in an inspiring rural setting like none other in the region.

Weddings

Surrounded by the lush bamboo forest and boundless mountains, Valley Stables is the destination in China for a unique and romantic wedding. Whether on The Big Lawn with horses and the Stables as a backdrop or in the Amphitheatre surrounded by lush greenery, experience the finest wedding services with a range of tailor-made options.

School Activities

Students get away from city life and immerse into nature for a different take on education. Focused on environment and sustainability, fresh air and tranquility, students leave with a new appreciation of mother nature.
